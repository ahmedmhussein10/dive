Are the websites SEO-Optimized ?

Yes, the websites come out not only mobile-optimized, interactive, and intuitive but also fully SEO-optimized, meaning that your potential clients will be able to easily find your product or service just by searching it up. I will be working on SEO simultaneously with developing the website itself. Thus, you will get both an amazing website and well-thought out search engine optimized content.

What is the cost of building a website ?

The cost of building a website ranges from $2000 to $10,000 based on the complexity of the project.
Before thinking about whether this fits your budget, let me tell you some of the results of a poor website design:

→ high maintenance
→ employees do not want to show the website to their friends
→ customers leave feeling frustrated and dissatisfied
→ it is impossible to find the website on any search engine
